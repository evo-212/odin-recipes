Garlic and Herb Roasted Chicken Recipe

This is a recipe for a garlic and herb roasted chicken. It is a simple recipe that is easy to make and is a great way to cook a chicken. Great for a family dinner or for meal prep!

Key Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Garlic
  • Herbs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Baking Instructions

  1. First, preheat your oven to 475 degrees fahrenheit
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper
  3. Rub the mixture all over the chicken breast
  4. Place the chicken in a roasting pan
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through
  6. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before slicing

Serving Suggestions

This chicken is great with a side of roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes. It is also great for meal prep and can be easily reheated in the microwave.
